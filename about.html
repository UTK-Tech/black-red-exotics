Why Black & Red

Black & Red Exotics started with a simple frustration: exotic rentals in LA all felt the same — stock cars, hidden fees, and showroom attitude. We built the opposite. Every car in our fleet is fully modified — custom exhausts, tuned horsepower, forged wheels, carbon accents — so what you drive feels personal, not rented.

We deliver anywhere in Los Angeles and Orange County, verify your insurance for you, and price straight with zero hidden fees. That's why over 200 drivers have rated us five stars.
